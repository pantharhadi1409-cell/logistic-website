Privacy Policy

Your trust is the foundation of our business. NexLogistics is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data.

Last updated: October 15, 2023
Jump to Section

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using our logistics services, website, or mobile applications.

Personal Data

  • Account Details: Name, email address, phone number, and company name when you register for a NexLogistics account.
  • Shipment Data: Sender/Receiver details, addresses, package descriptions, and weight provided for quotes or bookings.
  • Payment Data: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely via third-party providers), and tax ID.

Technical Data

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and operating system.
  • Log Data: Pages visited, time spent, and errors encountered on our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We utilize your data to provide, improve, and secure our global logistics services.

  • Service Provision: To process bookings, calculate rates, and arrange transport of your cargo.
  • Tracking: To provide real-time updates on the location and status of your shipments.
  • Communication: To send invoices, booking confirmations, and operational updates via email or SMS.
  • Security: To verify identity and prevent fraud or unauthorized access to your account.
  • Analytics: To analyze usage trends and improve our website functionality and user experience.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

NexLogistics does not sell your personal data. We only share your information under specific circumstances:

Third Parties

  • Service Providers: Customs brokers, airlines, shipping lines, and trucking partners necessary to complete your shipment.
  • Payment Processors: Financial institutions to process payments securely.
  • Legal Authorities: Government bodies for customs clearance and regulatory compliance.

Global Transfers: As a global logistics provider, your data may be transferred to countries outside your country of residence to facilitate your shipments. We ensure adequate protection in accordance with GDPR and other applicable laws.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing.

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit between your device and our servers.
  • Access Control: Strict authentication procedures for internal staff access to customer data.
  • Storage: Secure cloud storage with regular backups and vulnerability scanning.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data, subject to legal retention requirements (e.g., tax records).
  • Objection: Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website.

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the site to function (e.g., logging in, remembering preferences).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site to improve performance.
  • Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements to you across the web.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, though doing so may limit functionality.

Questions about your privacy?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our Data Protection Officer.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about sea freight.

FCL (Full Container Load) means you book an entire container for your goods. LCL (Less than Container Load) means your goods are consolidated with other shippers' goods in one container. FCL is usually cheaper per cubic meter for large volumes, while LCL is better for smaller shipments.

Transit times vary by route. Typically, trans-Pacific (Asia to USA) takes 15-25 days. Trans-Atlantic (Europe to USA) takes 7-14 days. Please allow extra time for customs clearance at both ends.

Incoterms (International Commercial Terms) define the responsibilities of buyers and sellers for the delivery of goods under sales contracts. Common terms include FOB (Free on Board) and CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight).
